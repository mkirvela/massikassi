WTFAQ

Service

Massikassi is a service to easily share expenses, when you want to keep it fair. Like going out, having some drinks, some food and sharing cabs. Or you got a few roomies and occasionally someone has to get more mayo.

Logic

Massikassi works "against the pot" - meaning that we bundle the expenses of a single event, then calculate how you can resolve the debts easily and with the least amount transactions. Check out our examples for details.

Precision

This is not a banking service. We round up payments to the second decimal (0.01) and it is entirely possible someone might end up paying a few cents more than someone else.

Free

The basic Massikassi service is free.

Why not use...

There are other services and you might want to check them out! We couldn't find one that really works for us.

The name

Massikassi is a way of saying 'moneybag' and can be used to refer to the person that every other sucker owes money to. It's Finnish slang.

Payment 1

Urz the Impatient quickly grew tired of the haggling, up-ending the pouch with all the electrum coins on the ironwood desk of the merchant, who skillfully hid her mirth behind her silken scarf. "May your bones soon pale under the three suns", he spat, and stomped away with a box containing three live compass beetles. He regretted the cost already, but the beetles would be sorely needed to cross the Mirror Plains. Surely the others will agree?

A box of compass beetles $105 - Urz the Impatient +70, Twirlduck -35, Baron Samedi -35

Payment 2

Twirlduck was miserable, being painfully aware now that playing skull dice against the sisters of the Quantum Order is a folly - especially after smoking some dried toad poo with the locals. Last night it had all seemed like a thing to do. Having lost all of her cash, she was now forced to borrow some from the Baron, who - the indignation! - would not take a word of an honest duck, but required a proper beak mark into some incomprehensible parchment. Now she had a horrible hangover AND some ink on her beak.

A personal loan of $48 - Baron Samedi +48, Twirlduck -48.

Payment 3

Baron Samedi again had challenges to perceive which world-thread exactly its consciousness was inhabiting, mainly because it was just too damn bored to pay attention. The one where the sentient viruses had enslaved the mankind? The one with the robotic tyrant pope? Nope, neither of those have ice cream and look, here's a barrow advertising just that! Must be the one where that silly duck princess just traded her soul to a handful of coins. Oh well, could as well get some ice cream for everyone...

Ice cream $6 - Baron Samedi +4, Twirlduck -2, Urz the Impatient -2
